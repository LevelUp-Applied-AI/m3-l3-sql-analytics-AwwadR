# KPI Brief — Levant Tech Solutions

## KPI 1: Employee Utilization Rate

**Definition:** Percentage of employees assigned to at least one project
**Source:** employees and project_assignments tables
**Current value:** 70%
**Interpretation:** Around 70% of employees are actively assigned to projects, while 30% are unassigned, indicating available workforce capacity.

## KPI 2: Total Salary by Department

**Definition:** Total salary expenditure per department
**Source:** Q2 query
**Current value:** Enginreering has the highest total salary (938,000), followed by Research (558,000)
**Interpretation:** Engineering is the most resource-intensive department, indicating high investment in technical roles.

## KPI 3: Highest Paid Employees per Department

**Definition:** Top-paid employee in each department
**Source:** Q3 query
**Current value:** Example: Engineering → Mazen Bishara (120,000), Research → Adel Hindawi (110,000)
**Interpretation:** Identifies key high-value roles and leadership positions across departments.

## KPI 4: Unassigned Employees
**Definition:** Number of employees not assigned to any project
**Source:** Q7 query
**Current Value:** 18 employees
**Interpretation:** A significant number of employees are not currently assigned to projects, which may indicate underutilization or upcoming resource availability.
